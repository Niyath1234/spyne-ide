Tl;DR

UPI Lite will allow the system to scale massively and also improve the overall SRs and speed of payments

Context:-

  • NPCI has recently launched UPI Lite framework to achieve the below-

    • Enhanced customer experience by bypassing UPI PIN for transactions under ₹200

    • Increased success rate for under ₹200 transactions by reducing a leg in transaction process

    • Decluttering customer bank account and reducing load on emitter bank infra for scalability

  • We need to implement this because -

    • Better customer experience with improved success rate & transaction speed

    • Be among the first to hit market- this will help in +ve marketing and increasing market share

    • Implementing this is mandatory from NPCI perspective

PRD/Figma Link:-

  1. UPI Lite

Metrics to track:-

  1. % of active customers who transact for under ₹200 transactions adopting UPI Lite

  2. Success rate of UPI lite transactions
